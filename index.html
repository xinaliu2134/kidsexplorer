A verified dataset of 7,600+ family venues, ready to plug in.

一份 7,600+ 亲子场所的干净数据，为你的产品所用

If you run a travel app, a family platform, a tourism board, or a research project and you need a clean, cross-checked dataset of family-friendly venues across 19 European countries — with verified coordinates, opening hours, categories, and Chinese-language support flags — you can license it directly.

旅行 App、亲子平台、旅游局、学术研究需要干净的亲子场所数据？我们提供涵盖欧洲 19 国、经三重校验的数据集——含精确坐标、营业时间、品类与中文友好标签——可直接授权使用。

    亲子旅行/行程 App：调用接口 + 月度更新，省去爬虫维护。

    旅游局/目的地营销机构：按国家或地区定制导出，用于门户、印刷指南、宣传物料。
  • 03
    Research & family-tech teams
    CSV / JSON snapshot with categories, age ranges, and Chinese-support flags.
    学术研究 / 亲子科技团队：CSV / JSON 快照，含品类、年龄段、中文友好标签。
One.

Verified coordinates, real opening hours.

Every venue is cross-checked against OpenStreetMap, Nominatim, and the brand's own website — so the pin is where the place is, and the hours are this week's.

每个地点都经 OSM、Nominatim 和品牌官网三重校验——图钉落在真实位置，营业时间每周更新。

Two.

Bilingual, by design.

Everything in English and 中文, with Chinese-language support indicators on venues that offer signage, audio guides, or staff who can help in Mandarin.

全站中英双语，并标注哪些场所提供中文标识、中文语音导览或会讲中文的工作人员。

Three.

Free forever, no ads, no accounts.

Built by a Dutch-Chinese mom for families planning weekend trips and school holidays. No pop-ups, no sign-ups — just open the map and go.

一位荷兰华人妈妈为家庭出游打造。没有广告、不用注册、不需账号——打开地图就能用。
