Best theme parks in France for families法国最值得带娃去的主题乐园

France has more world-class theme parks than any other country in Europe — from Disney magic and Gaulish roller coasters to the park just crowned the world's best. Here are the six that are worth building a family trip around.

法国拥有全欧洲最多的世界级主题乐园——从迪士尼的魔法、高卢人的过山车,到刚被评为"全球最佳"的乐园。这 6 个最值得为它们专程安排一趟亲子行程。

  • Under-8s & first visit (低龄首选): Disneyland Paris

1. Disneyland Paris

Marne-la-Vallée, near Paris · ages 2+ · two parks

Europe's most-visited theme park resort needs little introduction: two parks — Disneyland Park with the fairy-tale castle and classic dark rides, and Walt Disney Studios Park, in the middle of a multi-year expansion. For children under 8 it's hard to beat: character meet-and-greets, parades, baby-care centres and stroller rental everywhere. Just 35 minutes from central Paris by RER A.

欧洲游客量第一的主题乐园度假区:两个园区——有童话城堡和经典暗乘项目的迪士尼乐园,以及正在大规模扩建的华特迪士尼影城。对 8 岁以下的孩子来说几乎无可替代:卡通人物见面会、花车巡游、母婴室和随处可租的婴儿车。从巴黎市中心坐 RER A 线只要 35 分钟。

Tip · 小贴士: plan 2 full days for both parks; book time-slotted character meets in the app at park opening. 两个园区建议安排两整天;开园时就在 App 里预约人物见面会。

2. Parc Astérix

35 km north of Paris · ages 4+ · coasters & comedy

France's cheeky answer to Disney, built around the Astérix comics: around 50 attractions across six themed worlds, with some of Europe's best wooden and inverted coasters for older kids — plus a dedicated toddler corner. New for 2026 is the completely rebuilt Egypt zone, headlined by La Descente du Nil water ride, and a summer drone-and-fireworks night show.

法国版的"迪士尼",以高卢英雄阿斯泰利克斯漫画为主题:6 大主题区约 50 个项目,既有欧洲顶级的木质和悬挂过山车,也有专门的幼儿区。2026 年全新改建的埃及区登场,主打水上项目"尼罗河漂流",夏季还有无人机+烟花夜场表演。

Tip: weekdays outside French school holidays are dramatically quieter. 避开法国学校假期的工作日去,人少非常多。

3. Futuroscope

Poitiers · ages 5+ · tech & future

Crowned the world's best amusement park in Tripadvisor's 2026 Travellers' Choice awards — ahead of every Disney park. Futuroscope is different: around 40 attractions built on cutting-edge effects, 4D cinemas, time-travel dark rides and spectacular tech-driven shows. It's the park where kids come out saying "how did they do that?", and it pairs perfectly with Puy du Fou two hours away.

在 Tripadvisor 2026 年"旅行者之选"中被评为全球最佳乐园——排在所有迪士尼乐园之前。Futuroscope 走的是另一条路:约 40 个项目全部围绕尖端特效——4D 影院、时空穿越暗乘、震撼的科技演出。孩子出来都会问"这是怎么做到的?"。距 Puy du Fou 两小时车程,很适合连玩。

Tip: stay for the evening lagoon show — it's included in the ticket. 留到晚上看湖上夜秀,门票已包含。

4. Puy du Fou

Vendée, western France · ages 6+ · live spectacle

No roller coasters at all — and repeatedly voted among the best parks on earth. Puy du Fou is a series of jaw-dropping live shows staged in historical villages: Roman chariot races in a real arena, Viking longboats rising from the lake, hundreds of birds of prey overhead. School-age kids and grandparents are equally spellbound. Most shows are in French, but multilingual translation headsets are available.

完全没有过山车——却屡次被评为全球最佳乐园之一。Puy du Fou 是一连串令人瞠目的实景演出:真实竞技场里的罗马战车赛、从湖中升起的维京战船、数百只猛禽从头顶掠过。学龄孩子和祖父母都会看得入迷。演出以法语为主,但提供多语言同传耳机。

Tip: book the Cinéscénie night spectacular months ahead — 2,500 performers, sells out fast. 夜场大秀 Cinéscénie 需提前几个月订票,2500 名演员,场场售罄。

5. Le PAL

Allier, Auvergne · all ages · rides + zoo

France's best-kept family secret: the only park in the country that combines a full amusement park (about 30 rides) with a real zoo of 700 animals, set in 50 hectares of countryside. Toddlers ride the carousel in the morning, watch sea lions at noon and meet giraffes after lunch. The safari-style lodges overlooking the savannah plain make for an unforgettable overnight stay.

法国家庭游的"宝藏乐园":全法唯一把完整游乐园(约 30 个项目)和拥有 700 只动物的真动物园合二为一的乐园,坐落在 50 公顷的乡野中。小朋友上午坐旋转木马、中午看海狮表演、下午喂长颈鹿。俯瞰草原的 Safari 风格度假屋,住一晚终生难忘。

Tip: it's rural — easiest by car, about 3.5 hours from Paris. 位置偏乡村,自驾最方便,距巴黎约 3.5 小时。

6. Nigloland

Dolancourt, Champagne · ages 1–10 · toddler heaven

A family-run park in the Champagne countryside that French parents consistently rank as the country's most toddler-friendly: 27 of its attractions are open to children under one metre tall. Baby-changing in every toilet block, highchairs and microwaves in every restaurant, stroller rental at the gate — the logistics of small-kid travel are simply solved here.

香槟地区的家族经营乐园,被法国父母公认为全国对幼儿最友好的乐园:27 个项目身高不足 1 米的孩子也能玩。每个卫生间都有尿布台,每家餐厅都有儿童椅和加热宝宝餐的微波炉,门口就能租婴儿车——带小娃出行的各种麻烦在这里都被解决了。

Tip: open April to early November; combine with a Champagne-region weekend. 每年 4 月至 11 月初开放,可和香槟产区周末游连起来。
