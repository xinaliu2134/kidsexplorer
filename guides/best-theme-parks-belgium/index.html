Best Theme Parks in Belgium for Kids

Four parks, four very different days out — from gentle rides for toddlers to coasters for teens, with an indoor backup for rainy days. Written for families visiting from anywhere. 比利时亲子乐园地图 · 四座乐园，覆盖学步宝宝到青少年，含雨天室内备选。

Belgium is small, so you can base yourself in one city and reach most of these parks within 60–90 minutes. Here is how the four big family parks compare — and which one fits your kids' ages, the weather, and your budget.

比利时国土小，住一个城市基本都能在 1–1.5 小时内到达。下面把四座主力亲子乐园按 孩子年龄、天气、预算 做了对比，帮你一眼挑对。

How to choose · 怎么选： Toddlers & under-7s → Plopsaland. Teens & thrill-seekers → Walibi. Animals + rides in one day → Bellewaerde. Worried about rain → Bobbejaanland (huge indoor playground). 学步/低龄选 Plopsaland，青少年选 Walibi，想一天看动物又玩机动选 Bellewaerde，怕下雨选 Bobbejaanland。
01

Plopsaland De Panne

普劳普斯兰 · 德帕讷
Best for toddlers & under-7s · 最适合低龄宝宝

The biggest Plopsa park and Belgium's top pick for little ones, built around Studio 100 characters (Maya the Bee, Bumba, K3). With 53 attractions and 7 coasters, there are plenty of gentle first rides plus a few that grow with the kids. It sits right by the North Sea coast, so you can pair a park day with the beach.

规模最大的 Plopsa 乐园，比利时低龄宝宝首选，围绕 Studio 100 卡通角色打造，53 个项目、7 条过山车，温和的"人生第一程"很多。紧邻北海海滩，可以一天乐园 + 一天赶海。

Tip · 贴士: book online for cheaper dated tickets; arrive at opening — the toddler rides queue up fast after 11am.
De Panne · West Flanders · stroller-friendly · indoor shows for rain · from ~€59

02

Walibi Belgium

瓦利比 · 比利时
Best for teens & thrill-seekers · 青少年/刺激控

Belgium's adrenaline park: big coasters like Kondaa, Vampire and Psyké Underground, plus the Aqualibi indoor water park next door (handy if the weather turns). Younger kids have their own zone, but the real draw here is for ages 10+.

比利时的刺激担当：Kondaa、Vampire、Psyké Underground 等大型过山车，隔壁还有 Aqualibi 室内水乐园（天气不好也能玩）。也有低龄区，但 10 岁以上玩得最尽兴。

Tip · 贴士: a combined Walibi + Aqualibi ticket is the smart buy if rain is forecast — you can switch indoors.
Wavre · near Brussels (~30 min) · best for ages 10+ · indoor water park on site · from ~€63

03

Bellewaerde

贝勒沃德
Best for mixed-age families · 不同年龄一起玩

Half theme park, half zoo: ride the Bengal Express past lions and tigers, walk through the monkey areas, then hop on family rides and a 4D cinema. Because it blends animals and attractions, it suits families whose kids span several ages — everyone finds something.

一半乐园一半动物园：坐孟加拉小火车看狮子老虎、穿过猴区，再玩家庭机动和 4D 影院。动物 + 机动的组合，特别适合孩子年龄跨度大的家庭，人人有得玩。

Tip · 贴士: do the animal areas first thing in the morning when the animals are most active and the ride queues are short.
Ieper (Ypres) · West Flanders · all ages · part-shaded, good for hot days · from ~€56

04

Bobbejaanland

博比扬兰
Best rainy-day backup · 最佳雨天备选

Around 50 attractions in the Antwerp countryside, from coasters and water slides to a Ferris wheel and shows. Its trump card for families is a 7,000 m² indoor play paradise plus a big outdoor playground — so a sudden downpour doesn't end the day.

安特卫普乡间约 50 个项目，过山车、水滑道、摩天轮、演出都有。对家庭的王牌是 7,000 平米室内游乐天地 + 大型户外游乐场——突然下雨也不怕，照玩。

Tip · 贴士: it's ~1h20 from Brussels by car; combine with a quiet countryside stay rather than a city base.
Lichtaart · Antwerp province · huge indoor play area · great for unpredictable weather
