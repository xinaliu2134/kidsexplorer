Best zoos & animal parks in Europe for families欧洲最值得带娃去的动物园

From the park crowned Europe's best to the oldest zoo in the world, these six animal parks are worth planning a whole trip around — pandas, penguins, immersive habitats and all.

从被评为"全欧最佳"的乐园,到世界上最古老的动物园,这 6 个动物园值得为它们专程安排一趟行程——熊猫、企鹅、沉浸式栖息地,应有尽有。

Quick pick · 一句话选

  • Best overall, immersive worlds (综合最强): Pairi Daiza (Belgium)
  • Giant pandas & huge variety (大熊猫): ZooParc de Beauval (France)
  • History & baroque setting (最古老): Schönbrunn (Vienna)
  • Most-visited, city-central (城中心): Zoo Berlin
  • Penguins & shows (企鹅·海洋秀): Loro Parque (Tenerife)

1. Pairi Daiza

Belgium · all ages · immersive worlds

Repeatedly voted Europe's best zoo, Pairi Daiza is part animal park, part walk-through wonderland: a Chinese garden with giant pandas, an African plain, an aquarium and aviaries spread over a former abbey estate. Plan a full day, or stay over in one of its themed lodges.

多次被评为全欧最佳动物园。Pairi Daiza 一半是动物园、一半是可穿行的奇境:有大熊猫的中式园林、非洲草原、水族馆和大型鸟舍,坐落在一座旧修道院庄园里。建议玩一整天,也可入住主题度假屋。

Tip · 小贴士: it's huge — wear comfortable shoes and start early. 园区很大,穿舒服的鞋、早点到。

2. ZooParc de Beauval

France (Loire Valley) · all ages · giant pandas

Consistently ranked among the world's best zoos, Beauval is home to giant pandas, koalas, white tigers and an enormous range of species across themed biomes and greenhouses. The aerial gondola and big-cat enclosures are firm family favourites.

常年入选全球最佳动物园之列。Beauval 有大熊猫、考拉、白虎,物种极其丰富,分布在各主题生态馆和温室里。空中缆车和大型猫科动物区是家庭最爱。

Tip: two days if you can — there's far more than one day holds. 有条件就安排两天,一天看不完。

3. Tiergarten Schönbrunn

Vienna, Austria · all ages · world's oldest zoo

Founded in 1752 in the grounds of Schönbrunn Palace, this is the oldest zoo in the world — and still one of the best, with giant pandas, a rainforest house and a baroque setting unlike anywhere else. Easy to combine with a palace visit.

1752 年建于美泉宫园林内,是世界上最古老的动物园——至今仍是最好的之一:有大熊猫、热带雨林馆,还有别处没有的巴洛克园林背景。可和美泉宫一起逛。

4. Zoo Berlin

Berlin, Germany · all ages · most-visited

The most-visited zoo in Europe, with around 20,000 animals from over 1,000 species — one of the richest collections anywhere. It sits right in the centre of Berlin, so it slots easily into a city break, and free kids' rally sheets keep young explorers busy.

欧洲参观人数最多的动物园,约 2 万只动物、1000 多个物种,是世界上最丰富的收藏之一。它就在柏林市中心,城市游里顺路就能去,还有免费的儿童寻宝单。

5. ARTIS Amsterdam Royal Zoo

Amsterdam, Netherlands · all ages · historic & central

One of the five oldest zoos in the world, ARTIS pairs elephants, giraffes and penguins with an indoor aquarium, planetarium and butterfly house — so it works rain or shine. Right in central Amsterdam and very walkable for little legs.

世界上最古老的五家动物园之一。Artis 既有大象、长颈鹿、企鹅,又有室内水族馆、天文馆和蝴蝶馆——晴天雨天都能玩。位于阿姆斯特丹市中心,小朋友走起来也不累。

6. Loro Parque

Tenerife, Spain · all ages · penguins & shows

On Tenerife in the Canary Islands, Loro Parque is one of the world's largest animal parks — famous for Planet Penguin with its huge penguin colony, plus sea lion and dolphin presentations and one of the biggest parrot collections on earth. A holiday highlight in its own right.

位于加纳利群岛的特内里费岛,Loro Parque 是全球最大的动物园之一——以"企鹅星球"的庞大企鹅群闻名,还有海狮、海豚表演,以及全球最大的鹦鹉收藏之一。本身就是度假的一大亮点。
