Rainy-day activities for kids in Amsterdam阿姆斯特丹雨天,带娃去哪儿

Dutch weather has opinions. When the rain sets in, these six indoor spots keep kids happy and dry — from a hands-on science museum to a ship you can climb aboard.

荷兰的天说变就变。下雨了也不怕——这 6 个室内去处能让孩子玩得开心又不淋湿,从动手玩的科学馆到能爬上去的大帆船。

Updated 2026 · Verified against OpenStreetMap + official sites · 经核对官网与 OSM

Quick pick · 一句话选

  • Hands-on science (动手科学): NEMO — ages 6–14
  • Art the whole family enjoys (全家看艺术): Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum
  • Animals indoors (室内看动物): ARTIS aquarium & planetarium
  • Climb a real ship (登船探险): Het Scheepvaartmuseum
  • Let toddlers run (放电): TunFun indoor playground

1. NEMO Science Museum

Oosterdok · ages 6–14 · hands-on

Amsterdam's most hands-on museum: five floors of experiments where everything is built to be touched, tested and tried. Kids explore electricity, light, water and chains of cause-and-effect, and the rooftop terrace is free even if you don't go in. Easily 3–4 hours.

阿姆斯特丹最"动手"的博物馆:五层楼的实验,所有东西都能摸、能试。孩子玩电、光、水和各种连锁反应,屋顶平台不进馆也能上去。轻松玩 3–4 小时。

Tip · 小贴士: tickets sell out on wet weekends — book ahead. 雨天周末票很抢手,提前订。

2. Rijksmuseum

Museumplein · all ages · art & history

The Netherlands' grand national museum — Rembrandt's Night Watch, dolls' houses and suits of armour. Pick up the family multimedia tour or a kids' treasure-hunt to keep younger visitors hooked; the building itself is a rainy-day spectacle.

荷兰的国家级大博物馆——伦勃朗《夜巡》、娃娃屋、盔甲。拿一份家庭多媒体导览或儿童寻宝单,小朋友也能逛得津津有味;光是建筑本身就值得雨天来。

3. Van Gogh Museum

Museumplein · ages 6+ · art

The world's largest Van Gogh collection, told as a story kids can follow — sunflowers, starry skies and the painter's own life. Family activity trails and a multimedia guide for children make a famously busy museum genuinely kid-friendly.

全球最大的梵高收藏,用孩子能听懂的故事串起来——向日葵、星空和画家的一生。家庭活动路线和儿童多媒体导览,让这座热门博物馆也很适合带娃。

Tip: timed-entry only — pick a slot in advance. 仅限预约时段入场,提前选好时间。

4. ARTIS — aquarium & planetarium

Plantage · all ages · animals indoors

ARTIS is mostly an outdoor zoo, but its Aquarium, Planetarium, Butterfly Pavilion and the neighbouring micro-museum Micropia are all indoors — so a downpour just means more time with the fish, stars and butterflies.

Artis 主要是室外动物园,但水族馆、天文馆、蝴蝶馆和隔壁的微生物博物馆 Micropia 都在室内——下大雨反而能多看会儿鱼、星星和蝴蝶。

5. Het Scheepvaartmuseum

Oosterdok · ages 4+ · maritime & interactive

The National Maritime Museum lets kids climb aboard a full-size replica of an 18th-century sailing ship and explore interactive exhibits about life at sea. It's right across the water from NEMO, so the two pair perfectly for a full rainy day.

国立海事博物馆能让孩子登上一艘 18 世纪帆船的等比复制品,玩关于航海生活的互动展项。它就在 NEMO 对岸,两个连着玩,雨天一整天刚好。

6. TunFun indoor playground

Mr. Visserplein · toddlers–age 12 · free play

Tucked into a former underpass, TunFun is a big underground indoor playground — slides, climbing, trampolines and toddler zones — where kids burn off energy while grown-ups have a coffee. The easiest "it's pouring and they need to run" option.

藏在一处旧地下通道里的大型室内游乐场——滑梯、攀爬、蹦床、还有幼儿区。孩子放电,大人喝咖啡。"外面瓢泼大雨、娃需要跑一跑"的最省心选择。
