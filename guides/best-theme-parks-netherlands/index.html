Best theme parks in the Netherlands for kids 荷兰最值得带娃去的主题乐园

From a 70-year-old fairytale wonderland to record-breaking roller coasters and a rain-proof indoor world — here are the six theme parks in the Netherlands most worth a family day out, sorted by who they suit best.

从开了 70 多年的童话乐园,到刷新纪录的过山车,再到雨天也能玩的室内乐园——下面这 6 个荷兰主题乐园最值得带娃去,并按"适合的年龄"帮你挑。

Updated 2026 · Verified against OpenStreetMap + each park's official site · 经核对官网与 OSM

Quick pick · 一句话选园

  • Toddlers & preschool (幼儿): Koningin Julianatoren, Madurodam
  • All ages / magical (全年龄·童话感): Efteling
  • Rainy day (雨天): Toverland (indoor halls)
  • Theme park + water slides (乐园+水上): Duinrell
  • Older kids & teens / thrills (大孩子·刺激): Walibi Holland

1. Efteling

Kaatsheuvel · all ages · fairytale

The Netherlands' most beloved park, open since 1952. Its Fairytale Forest (Sprookjesbos) enchants little ones, while immersive rides like Symbolica, Baron 1898 and the Python coaster keep older kids and adults happy. It's big and beautifully themed — easily a full day, or stay over at Efteling Village.

荷兰人气第一的乐园,1952 年开园。童话森林适合小小孩,Symbolica、Baron 1898、Python 过山车则照顾大孩子和家长。园区很大、布景精致,够玩一整天,也可住园区酒店。

Tip · 小贴士: arrive at opening — the park is huge and the marquee rides build queues by midday. 一开门就进,主力项目中午就开始排队。

2. Walibi Holland

Biddinghuizen · ages 10+ · thrills

The country's thrill capital. Award-winning coasters like Untamed and Goliath draw teens and adrenaline-loving older kids. Best skipped for little ones — this is a park for confident riders.

荷兰的"刺激担当"。Untamed、Goliath 等口碑过山车专为青少年和爱冒险的大孩子。小小孩不建议,适合敢玩的孩子。

Tip: check minimum height limits before you go if your child is on the cusp. 孩子身高临界的话,出发前先查各项目限高。

3. Toverland

Sevenum · all ages · rain-proof

Divided into richly themed worlds with a strong focus on atmosphere, Toverland suits all ages — and crucially, its huge themed indoor halls mean a wet day won't ruin your visit. A smart choice for unpredictable Dutch weather.

按主题分成多个区域、氛围感很强,老少皆宜;最大亮点是有大型室内场馆——下雨天照样玩。应对荷兰多变天气的聪明之选。

Tip: pair it with a rainy forecast — most of the fun stays open indoors. 看到预报有雨就选它,大部分项目在室内。

4. Duinrell

Wassenaar · families · park + waterpark

A rare two-in-one: an amusement park plus the Tiki Pool, one of Europe's largest indoor water-slide complexes. Great value for a mixed-age family — rides in the morning, slides in the afternoon — and it's close to The Hague and the coast.

少见的"二合一":游乐园 + Tiki Pool(欧洲最大室内水滑梯之一)。一家不同年龄都能玩——上午玩项目、下午玩水,离海牙和海边都近,性价比高。

Tip: bring swimwear — the waterpark is the highlight for many families. 记得带泳衣,水上乐园是很多家庭的最爱。

5. Madurodam

The Hague · younger kids · miniature & interactive

Not a ride park, but an iconic Dutch family outing: the whole of the Netherlands shrunk to miniature, with interactive exhibits that let kids "run" the country. Especially lovely for younger children and a gentle, educational half-day.

不是玩项目的乐园,而是经典的荷兰亲子去处:把整个荷兰缩成微缩模型,还有互动展项让孩子"经营"国家。特别适合年纪小的孩子,寓教于乐、半天刚好。

Tip: combine with nearby Scheveningen beach for a full day out. 可和附近的席凡宁根海滩搭着玩,凑成一整天。

6. Koningin Julianatoren

Apeldoorn · toddlers & under-10s

Purpose-built for the youngest visitors: gentle rides, play areas and a relaxed pace that won't overwhelm toddlers and preschoolers. If your crew is under 10, this is the easy, stress-free pick.

专为低龄孩子打造:温和的小项目、游乐区、节奏轻松,不会让幼儿"超载"。家里有 10 岁以下小朋友,选它最省心。
