Best Water Parks in Europe for Kids

Six standout family water parks — including indoor, year-round options that work even in winter or rain. Best ages, what to expect, and where each one is. Written for families travelling from anywhere. 欧洲亲子水上乐园 · 含全年开放的室内场，冬天下雨也能玩。

A water park can save a rainy day, a heatwave, or a whole winter break — but only if you pick the right one for your kids' ages and the season. Here are six of Europe's best for families, with the indoor / year-round ones flagged so the weather never decides for you.

水上乐园能拯救雨天、酷暑，甚至整个冬假——前提是按 孩子年龄和季节 选对。下面是欧洲六座最适合家庭的水上乐园，室内 / 全年开放 的都标注了，天气说了不算。

How to choose · 怎么选： Winter or rain → indoor & year-round (Tropical Islands, Therme Erding, Lalandia, Aqualibi). Sun & big thrills → Siam Park. Little ones → LEGOLAND Water Park. 冬天/雨天选室内全年开放的，要阳光和大刺激选 Siam Park，低龄宝宝选 LEGOLAND 水乐园。
01

Tropical Islands

热带岛屿 · 德国
Best indoor & all-ages · 最佳室内全年场

Europe's largest indoor water park, built inside a former airship hangar near Berlin and kept at a tropical 26°C all year. Sandy beaches, a lagoon, a rainforest, and slides for every nerve level — plus on-site lodging so you can stay over. Weatherproof, which makes it a brilliant winter or rainy-day call.

欧洲最大室内水乐园，建在柏林附近的飞艇机库里，全年恒温 26°C。沙滩、泻湖、雨林、各种胆量的滑道一应俱全，还能住在园内。完全不看天气，冬天/雨天的王牌之选。

Tip · 贴士: it's ~1 hr south of Berlin — combine with a city break, and book an overnight to use the park across two days.
Krausnick · near Berlin, Germany · indoor · year-round · all ages · on-site hotel

02

Siam Park

暹罗水上乐园 · 西班牙
Best for sun & thrills · 阳光+刺激首选

On Tenerife in the Canary Islands, Siam Park is regularly rated the world's best water park — Thai-themed, warm year-round, with adrenaline drops like the Tower of Power and a gentle Lost City zone built just for kids. The Canaries' mild winter means it's a true all-season pick if you want sun.

位于加纳利群岛特内里费岛，常年被评为全球最佳水上乐园——泰式主题、全年温暖，既有 Tower of Power 这种刺激大滑道，也有专为孩子打造的 Lost City 区。加纳利冬天也温和，想晒太阳的话四季皆宜。

Tip · 贴士: a combined Siam Park + Loro Parque ticket is great value if you have two days on the island.
Tenerife · Canary Islands, Spain · outdoor · warm year-round · thrills + kids' zone

03

Therme Erding

埃尔丁温泉水世界 · 德国
Best for winter & families who want both · 冬季+一站式

Near Munich, one of the world's largest thermal spa and water-park complexes: a big slide world (Galaxy) for the kids and teens, wave pools and palm-lined lagoons, plus thermal pools for the grown-ups. Indoor and outdoor zones mean it works in any season — especially winter.

慕尼黑附近，全球最大温泉 + 水乐园综合体之一：Galaxy 滑道世界给孩子和青少年，造浪池和棕榈泻湖，还有大人泡的温泉池。室内外都有，四季可玩，冬天尤其香。

Tip · 贴士: the slide world and the adults' thermal areas have different age rules — check which zones your kids can enter before you buy.
Erding · near Munich, Germany · indoor + outdoor · year-round · slides + thermal

04

LEGOLAND Water Park at Gardaland

乐高水上乐园 · 意大利加尔达
Best for younger kids · 最适合低龄宝宝

On Lake Garda, this LEGO-themed water park is purpose-built for little ones — shallow play areas, build-your-own-raft pools and gentle slides, alongside the main Gardaland park. The most toddler-friendly water option on this list.

位于加尔达湖畔的乐高主题水乐园，专为低龄宝宝设计——浅水玩乐区、可拼搭的"造船"水池和温和滑道，紧邻 Gardaland 主乐园。本榜单里最适合学步宝宝的水上选择。

Tip · 贴士: it's seasonal (summer) — pair it with a Gardaland day on a Lake Garda family holiday.
Castelnuovo del Garda · Lake Garda, Italy · outdoor · summer season · best for under-8s

05

Lalandia

拉兰迪亚 · 丹麦
Best Nordic resort stay · 北欧度假首选

Scandinavia's biggest indoor water park (the Aquadome), part of a family resort with self-catering cottages in Billund and Rødby. Warm pools, slides and a wave pool whatever the Nordic weather is doing — and Billund sits right next to LEGOLAND. A great winter or shoulder-season base.

斯堪的纳维亚最大的室内水乐园（Aquadome），属于带自助小木屋的家庭度假村（比隆和勒兹比）。无论北欧天气如何都有温水池、滑道和造浪池——而且比隆紧挨乐高乐园。冬季或淡季的绝佳大本营。

Tip · 贴士: a cottage + park package over 2–3 nights usually beats day tickets, and pairs perfectly with LEGOLAND Billund.
Billund / Rødby · Denmark · indoor · year-round · resort with cottages

06

Aqualibi

阿夸利比 · 比利时
Best quick city add-on · 城市近郊半日

An indoor tropical water park right next to Walibi Belgium, just outside Brussels — slides, a wave pool and a lazy river, kept warm all year. Easy to reach for a half-day, and you can combine it with the Walibi theme park on a single visit.

布鲁塞尔近郊、紧邻 Walibi Belgium 的室内热带水乐园——滑道、造浪池、漂流河，全年恒温。半日就能玩，还能和 Walibi 主乐园一次搞定。

Tip · 贴士: a Walibi + Aqualibi combo ticket is the smart buy — switch indoors if the weather turns.
Wavre · near Brussels, Belgium · indoor · year-round · combine with Walibi
