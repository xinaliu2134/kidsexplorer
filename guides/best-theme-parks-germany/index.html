Best theme parks in Germany for families 德国最值得带娃去的主题乐园

Germany is home to some of Europe's very best theme parks — from a record-breaking resort with its own water world to a LEGO park built for little hands. Here are five that are most worth a family trip, sorted by who they suit best.

德国坐拥欧洲顶级的主题乐园——从自带水上世界的巨型度假区,到为小小孩量身打造的乐高乐园。下面这 5 个最值得带娃去,并按"适合的年龄"帮你挑。

Quick pick · 一句话选园

  • Younger kids 3–12 (低龄): LEGOLAND Deutschland
  • All ages / best overall (全年龄·综合最强): Europa-Park
  • Movie & TV characters (动画卡通迷): Movie Park Germany
  • Stunning theming, ages 6+ (氛围控·大孩子): Phantasialand
  • Thrills near Hamburg (汉堡周边·刺激): Heide Park

1. Europa-Park

Rust · all ages · Germany's largest

Germany's biggest theme park and a perennial pick for "best in Europe." Over 100 attractions are spread across themed areas modelled on European countries, so there's something for every age — gentle rides for toddlers, world-class coasters for teens. Next door, the Rulantica indoor water world makes it an easy two-day trip.

德国最大的主题乐园,常年入选"全欧最佳"。100 多个项目分布在以各欧洲国家为主题的园区,各年龄都有得玩——幼儿的温和项目、青少年的世界级过山车。隔壁还有 Rulantica 室内水世界,轻松凑成两天行程。

Tip · 小贴士: it's vast — plan two days, or pick your must-ride areas in advance. 园区很大,建议安排两天,或提前选好必玩区域。

2. Phantasialand

Brühl (near Cologne) · ages 6+ · spectacular theming

Not the biggest, but one of the most beautiful parks in the world, famous for its winding, intricately themed streets and immersive lands. It's a particular hit with kids aged 6–12 and teens who love both atmosphere and a thrill. Easy to reach from Cologne.

不是最大,却是全球公认最美的乐园之一,以蜿蜒街道和极致主题造景闻名。6–12 岁孩子和青少年最爱——既有氛围又有刺激。从科隆出发很方便。

Tip: the queues for headline rides are shorter early and late in the day. 热门项目早晚排队更短。

3. LEGOLAND Deutschland

Günzburg · ages 3–12 · built for younger kids

Purpose-built for families with younger children. MINILAND recreates famous European landmarks in astonishing LEGO detail, and there are water rides, mild coasters and live shows for the whole family. If your kids are roughly 3–12, this is one of the most genuinely satisfying days out in Germany.

专为低龄家庭打造。MINILAND 用乐高积木精细复刻欧洲地标,还有水上项目、温和过山车和现场表演。孩子大约 3–12 岁的话,这是德国最让人满意的一日游之一。

Tip: there's an on-site resort if you'd rather not drive back the same day. 园区有度假酒店,不想当天返程可以住一晚。

4. Heide Park

Soltau (Lüneburg Heath) · families & thrill-seekers

One of Germany's largest parks, with around 40 rides set in nearly 900,000 m² of landscaped grounds. It balances big thrills with family attractions, making it a strong all-rounder — and it's an easy day trip from Hamburg.

德国最大的乐园之一,约 40 个项目坐落在近 90 万平方米的园林里。既有大刺激,也有适合一家人的项目,综合性强;从汉堡出发一日游很方便。

Tip: pair with a Hamburg city break for an easy weekend. 可和汉堡城市游搭配,凑成轻松的周末。

5. Movie Park Germany

Bottrop · younger kids & character fans

A movie- and TV-themed park where kids can step into worlds built around characters like PAW Patrol and SpongeBob, with shows, entertainment and family-friendly coasters. A great pick if you have younger children who love their favourite cartoon heroes.

以电影电视为主题的乐园,孩子能走进 PAW Patrol、SpongeBob 等角色的世界,还有表演和适合全家的过山车。家里有爱看动画的小朋友,选它准没错。
